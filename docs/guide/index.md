# Autopilot Marketplace

Autopilot is an agent-led procurement platform designed for enterprise buyers evaluating robotic cleaning solutions. It replaces the traditional sales-driven discovery process with AI-guided conversations that help buyers find, evaluate, and approve the right products for their facilities.

## Target Users

- **Enterprise facilities managers** evaluating robotic cleaning equipment for large-scale deployments
- **Procurement teams** seeking structured product comparisons and ROI justification
- **Operations leaders** looking to modernize cleaning operations with robotics
- **Sales representatives** using the platform to engage and support prospective buyers

## Conversation Phases

Autopilot structures the buyer journey into three distinct phases:

### 1. Discovery

The AI agent conducts a guided conversation to understand the buyer's facility type, cleaning requirements, operational constraints, and budget parameters. Product recommendations are surfaced using RAG-powered search against a Pinecone vector database of robotic cleaning solutions.

### 2. ROI

Based on Discovery inputs, Autopilot generates ROI projections and visualizations that compare robotic solutions against current cleaning costs. This phase produces structured financial data that procurement teams can use internally.

### 3. Greenlight

The final phase consolidates the buyer's selections, ROI analysis, and configuration into an actionable procurement summary ready for executive approval.

## Key Features

- **AI discovery conversations** powered by GPT-4o for natural, context-aware dialogue
- **RAG-powered product search** using Pinecone to match buyer needs to relevant solutions
- **ROI visualization** with data-driven projections tailored to each buyer's inputs
- **Company accounts** for team-based evaluation and shared conversation history
- **Conversation history** so buyers can resume sessions and revisit prior recommendations

## Business Objectives

- Reduce time-to-evaluation for enterprise robotic cleaning purchases
- Provide buyers with unbiased, data-backed product recommendations
- Generate structured procurement artifacts that accelerate internal approval
- Enable sales teams to engage buyers with richer context from AI-led conversations

## Product Principles

- **Agent-first**: The AI agent drives the interaction, not static forms or catalogs
- **Structured output**: Every conversation phase produces actionable, structured data
- **Buyer autonomy**: The platform empowers buyers to self-serve without requiring sales calls
- **Rapid iteration**: Ship quickly, measure outcomes, and improve the agent's effectiveness continuously
